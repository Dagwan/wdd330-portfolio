/ our history

Dan is an online marketplace for users to buy and sell new & used items, as well as hire on demand home services. It all began in 2020, the year Covid-19 was declared a pandemic and most economies in different countries around the world were shut down. Getting access to products and service became scarce and nearly impossible, it was within this time, Dan Market Place saw the need for providing an alternative and seamless process of getting products and on demand home services when needed, and in a timely manner.

Not only that Dan Market Place was developed as an answer to the increasing demand for seamless process of shopping and hiring on demand home service on the same platform but also afford anyone who needs to have a digital footprint, an online presence for their business to showcase, we know that not everyone has access to funds or knowledge for developing a website or a mobile app to showcase their product or service skills to the internet community and world at large.

Also, Small and medium businesses face several challenges in access to mass consumers and obtaining adequate technology and infrastructure, they're struggling to gain benefits from the existing marketplaces, these platforms are providing them access to millions of consumers, yet many businesses struggle to stabilize on these marketplaces because of the stiff competition, limited budgets to compete with bigger brands, and high commission/subscription fees charged by these marketplaces.

Due to the increased need for affordable and reliable e-commerce solutions, we developed one of the only fully-featured, secure and powerful e-commerce platform Integrated with complete marketplace solution that allows these small and medium businesses overcome these technology and infrastructure challenges, allowing these small and medium businesses owners to launch their online furniture business.

Today, we're proud to empower individuals and small and business owners around the world. Everyone deserves an opportunity to have a digital foot print, an online presence for their business, and we're excited to see what you make with this tool.
